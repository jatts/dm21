Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

Discount Manager ("App") aapki privacy ki hifazat karna chahta hai. Ye policy batati hai ke hum kya data collect karte hain aur kaise use karte hain.

2. Data Collection

Local Data (Device par stored):

  • Scan history (sirf aapke device par)
  • Game progress — coins, level, total scans
  • Player profile — naam aur avatar (aapke device par)
  • App settings aur theme preference

Ye sab data sirf aapke device ki local storage mein save hota hai. Koi bhi server par upload nahi hota.

3. Camera Permission

App barcode scan karne ke liye camera access maangti hai. Camera sirf scanning ke waqt use hoti hai. Koi bhi photo save ya upload nahi hoti.

4. Internet Connection

App ko internet chahiye kyunki database aur app files online server se load hoti hain. Hum koi bhi personal data internet par nahi bhejtay.

5. Advertisements

Future mein app mein ads (AdMob) shamil ho sakti hain. Ads ke liye Google ki apni privacy policy apply hogi.

6. Third-Party Services

App Google Fonts aur FontAwesome icons use karti hai jo internet se load hoti hain.

7. Children

Ye app kisi bhi age group ke liye safe hai. Hum 13 saal se kam umra ke bachon ka koi personal data collect nahi karte.

8. Contact

Privacy se related koi sawal ho toh app developer se app store ke zariye rabta karein.

By using Discount Manager, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
Terms of Use

Last updated: June 2026

Discount Manager ek barcode scanning tool hai jo retail stores mein article discount information dekhne ke liye banaya gaya hai.

5. Changes

Hum kabhi bhi in terms ko update kar sakte hain. App ka continued use matlab aap updated terms se agree karte hain.
