Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

Discount Manager ("App") is provided by its developer as a free, ad-supported service. By using this App, you agree to this Privacy Policy. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights.

2. Information We Collect

a) Data stored locally on your device only:

  • Scan history and manually entered prices
  • Game progress — coins, level, total scans
  • Player profile — name and avatar
  • App settings and theme preferences

This data is stored only on your device and is not collected or uploaded to any server by the developer.

b) Data collected by third-party services:

The App uses the following third-party services which may collect data to identify you or for analytics and advertising purposes. Please review their respective privacy policies.

3. Advertising — Google AdMob

This App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements, including banner ads and rewarded video ads. AdMob may collect and use data including:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • App usage and interaction data

This data is used to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings:

Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

Google AdMob Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

4. Camera Permission

The App requires camera access to scan barcodes. The camera is used only during active scanning. No photos or videos are stored or transmitted.

5. Internet Access

The App requires an internet connection to load the product database and app interface hosted on GitHub Pages, and to display advertisements. No personal user data is sent over the internet by the developer.

6. Google Play Services

The App uses Google Play Services which may collect device and usage data. Privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

7. Log Data

In case of an error, third-party services (such as AdMob) may collect Log Data including your device IP address, device name, OS version, and timestamps. This is used for diagnostic purposes only.

8. Children's Privacy

This App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided personal data, please contact us to have it removed.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes are effective immediately upon posting. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact: rizwanchohdary@gmail.com
